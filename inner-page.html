Agrox Cinnamon App


Agrox Cinnamon App is a groundbreaking solution aimed at revolutionizing the Sri Lankan Ceylon Cinnamon industry. Our app addresses the industry's challenges and offers innovative features to enhance efficiency, quality, and profitability for growers, traders, and stakeholders. The app encompasses various components designed to provide a comprehensive solution to the challenges faced by the Ceylon Cinnamon industry.

01. Cinnamon Price Prediction

The Cinnamon Price Prediction component of the Agrox Cinnamon App utilizes advanced algorithms and market data to predict Ceylon Cinnamon prices. However, it's important to note that Cinnamon prices are not free-floating due to monopolistic practices in the industry, which lead to market fluctuations. To address this, we've developed a unique formula: ECP = (MO) X (100 – IW%). This formula calculates the Estimated Cinnamon Price (ECP) by factoring in the Model Output (MO) and Influence Weight Percentage (IW%). This formula has proven to be highly accurate, with over 97% precision in predicting prices within a margin of error of ±0.5 USD.

02. Grade Identification

Agrox's Grade Identification component employs cutting-edge technology to accurately classify Ceylon Cinnamon sticks into different grades. Our AI-based system scans, analyzes, and grades the cinnamon based on key parameters like color, texture, size, and aroma. This not only saves time but also ensures consistent quality, thus increasing trust and sales opportunities.

03. Farmer and Trader Networks

To bridge the gap between farmers and traders, we've created an extensive network that allows seamless interaction between them. This includes features like farmer profiles, product listings, direct communication, and a comprehensive directory. This network brings more transparency to the industry, helping farmers get fair prices and traders source quality products more easily.

04. Market Updates

Stay informed about the latest trends, news, and developments in the Ceylon Cinnamon industry. The app provides regular market updates to help users make informed decisions regarding their cinnamon-related activities.

05. Mobile Trading Platform

Agrox Cinnamon App features a user-friendly mobile trading platform, allowing traders and buyers to conduct business on-the-go. This platform includes features like secure transactions, real-time inventory tracking, and easy payment methods.

Agrox Cinnamon App is dedicated to transforming the Ceylon Cinnamon industry by making it more transparent, efficient, and profitable. By utilizing technology and innovation, we aim to create a sustainable future for Cinnamon growers, traders, and consumers.
